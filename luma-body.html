About Event

​🚀 LA Tech Week Kickoff: A Triple Header Day of Women’s Empowerment Movies on the Big Screen
Presented with the City of West Hollywood
Saturday, October 11

​Join us for an unforgettable day celebrating the power of women’s stories, voices, and impact. In partnership with the City of West Hollywood, we’re kicking off LA Tech Week with a cinematic triple feature that inspires, educates, and empowers.

​All Day PASS available NOW - Your All day pass includes all three films, free signed Show Her the Money book, networking breakfast and lunch, plus the after panels and awards!

​Individual Movie tickets will be available Sept 13th.

​This event is sponsored by the Sunset Marquis and Holland & Knight.

​​The first purchased 100 VIP tickets will receive a complementary copy of the beautiful #1 best-seller Show Her the Money coffee table book ($50 Value) Pick your book up the morning of the event. Book signing 9-10am with the movies Executive Producers: Emmy award winning actress Sharon Gless (Cagney & Lacey), Catherine Gray, Dawn Lafreeda and Diana Greshtchuk.

​

​🎬 The Films:

  • ​10am - Show Her The Money – Shining a light on the fact that women receive less than 2% of venture capital funding, this award-winning documentary demystifies the VC world and showcases visionary female founders. Special in-person appearance by Executive Producer & Cast Member, Emmy Award-winning actress Sharon Gless(Cagney & Lacey, Queer as Folk). Plus Award for actress Sharon Gless.

  • ​1pm - Lilly – The inspiring true story of Lilly Ledbetter, whose fight for equal pay led to the historic Lilly Ledbetter Fair Pay Act, signed into law by President Obama. Featuring Academy Award-nominated actress Patricia Clarkson. Plus Gai Gherardi Visionary Award.

  • ​3pm - Still Working 9 to 5 – A behind-the-scenes look at the making of the beloved classic 9 to 5 and the ongoing fight for workplace equality, featuring icons Lily Tomlin, Jane Fonda, and Dolly Parton. Plus iconic award recipients.

​💡 Why it matters:
These stories span business, politics, and pop culture—reminding us that real change is made when women’s voices are amplified.

​What to expect:

​9 – 1 0 am - Coffee and Bagels in the Lobby & Book signing With Actress/Producer Sharon Gless, Director Ky Dickens, Producers Catherine Gray, Dawn Lafreeda and Diana Greshtchuk, all featured in the book!

​10 am - SHOW HER THE MONEY and After Panel with Cast and Producers

​12 – 1 pm - Casual Lunch and Mixer in the Lobby

​1 – 3 pm - LILLY and After Panel

​3 – 5 pm - STILL WORKING 9 to 5 and After Panel

​Awards given after each movie!
